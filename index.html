Software Engineer & AI Researcher

Hello! My name is Raghav and I am a software engineer with a background in Neuromorphic AI research. My primary interests are in brain-inspired AI, specifically in the area of continual learning, plasticity mechanisms and spiking neural networks. I have worked on interdisciplinary teams as well as presented research findings in top conferences (NICE) during my time in graduate school at The University of Texas at San Antonio. My experience working in research has fostered a strong sense of collaboration as well as the ability to work independently. I really enjoy coding and building dashboards to clarify complex topics. I use this blog to post projects and explanations on research ideas.
