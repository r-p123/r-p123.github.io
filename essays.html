Spiking Neuron Pseudo-Derivation

Explaining how to engineer a spiking neuron from an artificial neuron. We start with a very basic artificial neuron and slowly add temporal dynamics to observe a neuron that changes over time instead of the traditional static behavior of artificial neurons. Includes visualization dashboard to observe temporal dynamics of a spiking neuron with different parameters.
